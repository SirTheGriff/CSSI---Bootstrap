Directions to CSSI

Directions from Philadelphia:

  • Follow I-76 W toward the sign for exits 328B-A / 327 for 202 to 422 W to King of Prussia.
  • Take exit 328A to 422 W / Swedesford Road.
  • Follow signs for Swedesford Rd.
  • At the traffic light go straight onto Guthrie Rd.
  • At the next light Creed’s Seafood is on your left. Turn left onto N Gulph Rd.
  • Take the very 1st right onto Freedom Business Center Dr.
  • Park at the first building on your right – we are on the 3rd floor.

Directions from Pennsylvania Turnpike:

  • Follow PA Turnpike to exit 326 to merge onto I-76 E toward Valley Forge/US-202/I-476/Philadelphia.
  • Take exit 327 for N Gulph Road toward Valley Forge.
  • Turn right onto Freedom Business Center Dr.
  • Park at the first building on your right – we are on the 3rd floor.
